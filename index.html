A Digital Hotel Key Card

  • Get a keycard by authenticating at the front desk
  • Card can be used by anyone who can get hold of it
  • Card can be revoked at any time
  • Has no meaning to the application using it
  • Doesn't include your personal information
  • Card can be used to access multiple doors
  • Card expires at the end of your visit
